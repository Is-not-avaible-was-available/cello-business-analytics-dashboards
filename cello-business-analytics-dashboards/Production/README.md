# Production Analytics Dashboard

## 📊 Overview
This dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of production data across multiple material hierarchies and product categories. It enables monitoring of production performance, identification of high-output products, and supports data-driven production planning.

---

## 🔹 Key Metrics
- Total Production (BUOM)
- Total Production (Sale UOM)
- Production by Finished Goods (FG)
- Production by Material Group (Level 1–5)

---

## 🔹 Analysis Performed
- Identified top-performing products based on production volume  
- Analyzed production distribution across Material Group 1 to Material Group 5  
- Compared production output across product categories and hierarchies  
- Evaluated contribution of different product segments to overall production  

---

## 🔹 Key Insights
- A few product categories contribute disproportionately to total production, indicating concentration risk  
- Certain material groups dominate production output, highlighting core business drivers  
- Long-tail products show significantly lower production volumes, suggesting optimization opportunities  
- Production trends can be aligned with sales demand to improve planning efficiency  

---

## 🔹 Features
- Hierarchical analysis across multiple material group levels  
- Interactive filtering (e.g., Month)  
- Product-level and category-level drill-down insights  
- Clear visualization of production distribution  

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Power BI (Dashboard Development)  
- Python (Data Cleaning & EDA using Pandas, NumPy)
